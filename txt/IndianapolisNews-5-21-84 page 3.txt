Indianapolis News May 21, 1884 pg. 3
Advertisement
Buffalo Bill's Wild West
Indianapolis Fairgrounds Monday and Tuesday May 26 and 27, afternoons only
Performance, rain or shine. The greatest show ever given!
America's National Entertainment
Buffalo Bill, King of the Border, aided by a score of heroes of the plains, among whom are Captain Bogardus, the world's champion shot, Buck Taylor, the Cowboy King, Major Frank North, the white chief of Pawnees, the Bogardus Boys, the Dead Shot Quartette, with cowboys, Indians, Mexicans, buffalo, elk, broncos, burros, and many other attractions.
Admission 50 cents; Children 25 cents. Gates open at 1 p.m. Performance begins at 2:30 p.m.
